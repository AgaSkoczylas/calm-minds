About Us

Calm Minds is an independent charitable organization created to promote mindfulness practice in order to improve people's wellbeing. We believe, mindfulness is freedom. It is a space for consciously responding to life in all its richness, instead of reacting to it in a habitual, automatic, and often unwise or unsupportive way for us as human beings. But it is also a RETURN HOME - TO YOURSELF! Mindfulness is a state of consciousness in which we focus on the present moment, with full engagement and attention, without judgment or evaluation. It is the ability to mindfully notice thoughts, emotions, and sensations, while disconnecting from negative thoughts and focusing on positive ones. Practicing mindfulness can help reduce stress, improve well-being, and increase focus and concentration. It can also help cope with difficult emotions and situations, by increasing self-awareness and the ability to focus on the present moment. We are here to show you where to start.
